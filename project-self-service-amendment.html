Overview

At Remitly, customers frequently needed to modify their money transfers after submission—whether changing recipient details, delivery methods, or transfer amounts. These amendments required contacting customer support, creating friction and increasing operational costs.

The Self Service Amendment project aimed to give users the power to make these changes themselves, improving the user experience while reducing support ticket volume.

Problem

Research revealed that:

  • ~20% of customer support tickets were related to transfer amendments
  • Users experienced frustration with wait times and process complexity
  • Support agents spent significant time on routine amendment requests
  • No self-service option existed for post-submission changes

Goals

  • Reduce amendment-related support tickets by 60%
  • Enable users to amend transfers within 5 minutes
  • Maintain regulatory compliance across all markets
  • Increase customer satisfaction scores

Process

Research & Discovery

I conducted stakeholder interviews, analyzed support ticket data, and performed user interviews to understand pain points. Key insights included:

  • Most amendments occurred within 30 minutes of transfer submission
  • Recipient name corrections were the most common request
  • Users expected the ability to self-serve, similar to e-commerce experiences

Design Exploration

I explored multiple design directions, considering:

  • Where to surface amendment options in the user journey
  • How to communicate amendment policies clearly
  • Validation flows to ensure accuracy
  • Confirmation and notification patterns

Prototyping & Testing

Created high-fidelity prototypes and conducted usability testing with 15 participants across 3 markets. Testing revealed the importance of:

  • Clear amendment eligibility indicators
  • Step-by-step guidance through the amendment process
  • Prominent confirmation messaging

Solution

The final design included:

  • Amendment Hub: Centralized access point from transaction details
  • Eligibility Checker: Real-time validation of what can be amended
  • Guided Flow: Step-by-step amendment process with inline validation
  • Confirmation System: Clear communication of changes with email/push notifications

Results

  • 65% reduction in amendment-related support tickets (exceeded goal)
  • Average amendment completion time: 3.5 minutes
  • Customer satisfaction score increased by 18 points
  • Successfully launched in 8 markets with full regulatory compliance

Learnings

This project reinforced the importance of:

  • Balancing user autonomy with regulatory requirements
  • Cross-functional collaboration with compliance and operations teams
  • Progressive disclosure to manage complex scenarios
  • Continuous iteration based on support ticket trends
