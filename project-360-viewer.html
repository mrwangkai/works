CarMax Web

360° Car Viewer

Patented interactive vehicle viewing technology enabling customers to virtually inspect cars online with transparency and confidence

Role

Senior Product Designer

Timeline

8 months

Team

3 Designers, 8 Engineers, 2 PMs, 1 Researcher

Platform

Web (Desktop & Mobile)

Overview

Building trust in online used car sales requires transparency and detailed visibility into vehicle condition. Customers need to assess a car's condition from home before committing to a major purchase.

The 360° Car Viewer provides customers with an immersive, interactive experience to virtually inspect every angle of a vehicle—both exterior and interior—helping them make confident purchase decisions remotely.

Opportunity

Online used car shopping faces a fundamental trust challenge:

  • Customers want to thoroughly assess a used car's condition before purchasing, but can't physically inspect vehicles when shopping online
  • Static photos don't provide enough detail or context to evaluate vehicle quality and condition
  • Trust concerns around vehicle condition are a primary barrier to completing online purchases
  • Competitors offering in-person inspection or limited photo galleries created a disadvantage for pure online shopping
  • Without transparency, customers default to visiting dealerships, reducing online conversion

Goals

  • Build customer trust and confidence in assessing vehicle condition remotely
  • Increase transparency in online used car sales
  • Provide a differentiated, innovative experience that competitors cannot easily replicate
  • Enable customers to make informed purchase decisions from home
  • Increase online purchase conversion rates

Process

User-Centered Discovery

Conducted research to understand customer needs and pain points:

  • Interviewed online car shoppers about their concerns and hesitations with remote purchases
  • Observed in-person vehicle inspections to understand what details customers examine
  • Analyzed customer service inquiries related to vehicle condition questions
  • Mapped the mental model customers use when evaluating used cars

Technology Exploration

Partnered with engineering and operations to develop capture and delivery solutions:

  • Researched 360-degree photography technologies and capture equipment
  • Designed studio capture process for consistent, high-quality vehicle photography
  • Optimized image processing and loading for web performance
  • Developed scalable workflow for thousands of vehicles

Interaction Design

Designed intuitive controls and feature highlights:

  • Drag-to-rotate interaction for smooth 360° exploration
  • Hotspot system to highlight key features, new parts, and areas of interest
  • Interior and exterior views for comprehensive inspection
  • Mobile-optimized touch gestures for on-the-go browsing

Testing & Refinement

Conducted usability testing and iterative refinement to validate the experience and identify improvements before launch.

Solution

The 360° Car Viewer provides a comprehensive interactive inspection experience:

  • Full 360° Rotation: Drag or swipe to rotate around the vehicle, viewing it from every angle
  • Exterior & Interior Views: Seamless toggle between outside and inside views for complete visibility
  • Interactive Hotspots: Clickable markers highlighting key features, recent repairs, and new parts
  • High-Resolution Detail: Studio-quality photography captured in controlled lighting for consistent, clear imagery
  • Smooth Performance: Optimized image loading ensures fast, responsive interaction across devices
  • Available for Studio Vehicles: Deployed for all CarMax studio-captured vehicles, providing a consistent premium experience

Patent Recognition: The innovative technology and approach earned Patent #10845943, protecting the unique implementation.

Results

  • Increased customer trust and confidence in assessing vehicle condition remotely
  • Higher engagement on vehicle detail pages with 360° viewer compared to standard photo galleries
  • Competitive differentiation as a first-mover in interactive vehicle viewing
  • Patent award (Patent #10845943) protecting the innovative technology
  • Positive customer feedback highlighting transparency and detail as key decision factors
  • Marketing asset featured in campaigns to attract online shoppers
  • Industry recognition as an innovative approach to building trust in online used car sales

Learnings

Key takeaways from this innovative project:

  • Transparency builds trust: Providing detailed, honest visibility into product condition—even flaws—increases customer confidence more than hiding imperfections
  • Innovation requires cross-functional collaboration: Success depended on design, engineering, operations, and photography teams working in sync to create both the experience and the content pipeline
  • Performance is part of the experience: Smooth, responsive interactions were essential to making the viewer feel premium and trustworthy
  • Operational scalability matters: Designing the studio capture workflow was as important as designing the interface—both needed to scale across thousands of vehicles
  • First-mover advantage: Launching a differentiated feature early created a competitive moat and positioned CarMax as an innovator in the space
  • Protection through patents: Securing intellectual property protection ensured the investment in innovation maintained its competitive value
