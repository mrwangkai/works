Shipped Experience

The finance-based shopping experience is currently live on CarMax.com, enabling customers to shop based on their approved financing terms and monthly payment preferences.

The Challenge

Many CarMax customers apply for financing before shopping for vehicles, but the existing experience didn't effectively connect financing approval to vehicle selection. This disconnect resulted in customers being uncertain which vehicles fit their budget, leading to abandoned purchases.

Key Finding: Customers with approved financing showed lower-than-expected conversion rates, often browsing vehicles outside their approved budget range or struggling to understand how their financing terms translated to specific vehicle options.

Goals

Redesign the post-financing-approval experience to seamlessly connect customers with vehicles that fit their budget and approved terms.

Target Impact: Improve app-to-sales conversion by making it effortless for customers to find and purchase vehicles within their financing approval.

The Solution

I redesigned the car shopping experience to center around financing and monthly payments:

  • Payment-first browsing: Show monthly payment prominently on every vehicle card, making budget comparison effortless
  • Approval-aware filtering: Guide customers to vehicles within their approved financing range with clear visual indicators
  • Unified car card component: Created consistent vehicle presentation across web, mobile, and in-store touchpoints
  • Clear post-approval flow: Streamlined journey from financing approval to vehicle selection with contextual guidance

Results

The finance-based shopping redesign delivered meaningful business and customer impact:

  • Improved app-to-sales conversion for customers with approved financing
  • Increased customer confidence in finding vehicles within their budget range
  • Reduced post-approval drop-off by connecting financing directly to vehicle selection
  • Reusable design system ‚Äî Car card component adopted across multiple platforms and touchpoints

Learnings

Key takeaways from this project:

  • Payment-first mindset aligns with customer behavior ‚Äî Most customers think in monthly payments, not total price
  • Cross-platform consistency drives trust ‚Äî Unified car card component created seamless experience across web, mobile, and in-store
  • Testing with real customers reveals edge cases ‚Äî In-store observation uncovered financing scenarios not captured in data
  • Design systems accelerate future work ‚Äî Reusable components enabled faster iteration and consistent experiences
