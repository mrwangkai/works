The Challenge

At Remitly, customers frequently needed to modify their money transfers after submission—whether changing recipient details, delivery methods, or transfer amounts. These amendments required contacting customer support, creating friction and increasing operational costs.

Key Finding: ~20% of customer support tickets were related to transfer amendments, with users experiencing frustration with wait times and process complexity.

The Solution

I designed Self Service Amendment – empowering customers to modify transfers independently with:

  • Amendment Hub: Centralized access point from transaction details
  • Eligibility Checker: Real-time validation of what can be amended
  • Guided Flow: Step-by-step amendment process with inline validation
  • Confirmation System: Clear communication of changes with email/push notifications

Results

Self Service Amendment delivered strong results across all metrics:

  • 65% reduction in amendment-related support tickets (exceeded goal of 60%)
  • 3.5 minutes average completion time, well within the 5-minute target
  • +18 points customer satisfaction increase
  • Successfully launched in 8 markets with full regulatory compliance

Learnings

Key takeaways from this project:

  • Autonomy with guardrails — Balancing user empowerment with regulatory compliance
  • Cross-functional is key — Close collaboration with compliance and operations enabled success
  • Progressive disclosure works — Simplified complex scenarios without overwhelming users
  • Listen to support data — Support ticket trends guided continuous iteration
