Shipped Experience

The Passbook cash deposit experience is currently live on Remitly's mobile app, enabling customers to add funds to their digital wallet at thousands of retail locations.

The Challenge

Remitly serves 44 million immigrants in the U.S., but many lack access to traditional banking. According to a 2016 FDIC report, 13% of immigrants were in unbanked households, while another 26% were underbanked—meaning nearly 40% of Remitly's target audience faced barriers to using existing money transfer services that required bank accounts or debit cards.

Key Finding: Existing Remitly services required bank accounts or debit cards for funding, excluding millions of cash-preferred customers who needed alternative ways to access digital money transfer services.

Goals

Design a digital wallet solution that serves unbanked and underbanked immigrants by providing full banking services—including money transfers, debit card, and cash deposits—without requiring a traditional bank account.

Target Impact: Expand financial access for 44 million immigrants in the U.S., enabling cash-preferred customers to participate in digital money movement.

The Solution

I designed the end-to-end cash deposit experience integrated with retail partner networks:

  • Location finder: Map-based interface showing nearby retail partners accepting cash deposits
  • Barcode generation: Dynamic QR codes and barcodes for cashiers to scan at point of sale
  • Real-time confirmation: Instant notification when deposit is processed and funds are available
  • Transaction history: Clear record of all cash deposits with receipt details
  • Multi-language support: Localized experience for diverse customer base

Results

Passbook delivered measurable business and customer impact:

  • 50% improvement in account funding: Increased conversion from account creation to first funding from 40% to 60%
  • 97% retention rate: Achieved exceptional month-over-month retention for primary users (8+ debit card transactions in 2nd month)
  • 76% payback period reduction: Improved from 122 months to 29 months, dramatically accelerating business sustainability
  • 40s% → 50s% primary account conversion: More customers became highly engaged after their first deposit
  • Streamlined onboarding: 70% of customers can complete KYC with just one document, with full onboarding in under 5 minutes
  • $40 LTV increase: Converting unbanked to banked customers increased lifetime value by ~$40 per customer

Learnings

Key takeaways from this project:

  • Design for cash-first users — Interface patterns that work for banked users may not translate to cash-preferred customers who think differently about money movement
  • Physical-digital bridge requires clarity — Clear instructions for retail interactions prevent confusion and failed transactions
  • Localization goes beyond translation — Cultural context shapes how customers understand and trust cash deposit processes
  • Retail partner constraints inform design — POS system limitations and cashier workflows directly impact UX decisions
